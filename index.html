About

I am a postdoctroal researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Korea University (DMIS Lab). My research area lies in natural language processing and machine learning. I am currently focusing on building real-time open-domain question answering models and solving biomedical NLP tasks. I was a Ph.D student at Korea University advised by Prof. Jaewoo Kang.
