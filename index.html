About

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Korea University (KU DMIS Lab). My research areas are mostly based on natural language processing and machine learning. Specifically, I'm interested in real-time reading comprehension models and biomedical text understanding. I was a PhD student at Korea University advised by Prof. Jaewoo Kang. During my PhD, I did an internship at NAVER Clova where I developed BioBERT and DenSPI.
